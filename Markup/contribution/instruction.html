instructions

Follow these steps to make a successful contribution with your ETH to hoard smart contract to buy HRD tokens. First copy Hoard contribution smart contract's address which can be seen after confirming conditions and clicking button continue on hoard contribution page.

IMPORTANT NOTE: make sure you have copied the right address by going to etherscan.io and pasting Hoard contract address there. You should see Hoard token name, reputation: OK and Official Links: pointing to Hoard official websites.

Contribute with MEW

  1. Go to MEW https://www.myetherwallet.com/ - Make sure the site is secured with https and green lock displays the certificate. Always check the URL and look for MYETHERWALLET LLC [US] Certificate up there. Look for https://www.myetherwallet.com/. Be safe & secure.

  2. Go to "Send Ether and Tokens" tab in the top panel and chose the way to access your Ether account.

  3. After you have succesfully unlocked your account you will see a text field "To Address". Paste Hoard contribution smart contract's address which can be seen after confirming conditions and clicking button Continute on hoard contribution page.

    IMPORTANT NOTE: make sure you have pasted the right address by going to etherscan.io and pasting Hoard contract address there. You should see Hoard token name, reputation: OK and Official Links: pointing to Hoard official websites.

  4. Chose amount of ETH you would like to contribute. We only accept ETH in the contribution.

  5. Set gas limit above 90000 to proritise your transaction.

  6. Pressing "Generate transaction" will prepare transaction to be send.

  7. Blue button "Send transaction" will send the transaction to the Ethereum network.

  8. After you sent your transaction you can check status of the transaction with etherscan.io, just press the button "Check TX Status".

  9. Wait for confirmations. Hooray you have successfully send ETH to the Hoard contract!

  10. To see how much HRD tokens you will get you have to wait until the crowd sale ends.

  11. Go to MEW, unlock your account, add custom token, paste Hoard contract address, name it HRD,in decimal points field enter 18 then press "Save".

  12. You will now see function "finalizeCrowdfunding" use it to finalize your contribution.

  13. you can check your HRD balance using curPubHRD4ETHVal function on the HRD token or go to contribution site and check your balance there.

FAQ

How can I take part in contribution and get some HRD tokens.

  • You can take part in contribution if you have some ETH on your ethereum account. Please follow the Instructions for Contribution.

What can I purchase HRD tokens with.

  • We only accept ETH in this contribution.

How much Hoard tokens will I get for my Ether.

  • It will depend on the amount of total contributions. We are looking to collect fixed number of ETH for this crowd sale. If we receive an amount higher then the amount we are looking for we will simply equally scale all the contributions and keep only what we need to develop Hoard.

When and how can I check my HRD token balance.

  • You can check you hoard balance by adding Hoard contract token as a custom token in your wallet and calling a function finalizeCrowdfunding on it. Then call curPubHRD4ETHVal or visit contribution after the crow sale is over.

How long will the crowd sale last.

  • Crowd sale will run for 4 weeks. The exact date will be announced on our contribution page.
